About Us

Once upon a time, the founders of FARM Florist were hardworking and passionate florists employed by other florist companies in Singapore. Bianca, Esther, Samantha, and Tiffany, got together one day and realised that they all had one thing in common â€“ they could not accept what their bosses were doing with their companies.

A few factors plague the florist industry in Singapore. These factors include dishonesty, frequent late deliveries, cost cutting by making bouquets smaller than advertised, or cost saving by sending out flowers that do not look fresh at all. As an employee, a florist who is highly passionate in creating smiles may often feel helpless and depressed.

These four ladies, with a combined experience of more than a century, wanted to stand up against these common practices. They believed that they could change the industry one bouquet at a time. After all, flowers usually symbolise love, gratitude, care, and respect.

What started out as a home business venture would later see them start their first floral studio in Toa Payoh where they would happily curate amazing floral arrangements daily. They worked at least 12 hours a day, 7 days a week, handling all tasks from customer service, to flower making, to logistics.

Today, with the help of some investors, who clearly saw much value and potential in them, FARM Florist is arguably the largest online florist and flower delivery in Singapore. This is what you get when you get a group of experienced and passionate florists, bursting with energy, backed with decent financial support.

With certain operational features like the FARM Florist Money Back Guarantee, 24/7 customer service, Live Order Tracking, and Lowest Cost Guarantee, it is unlikely anyone else in Singapore would be able to beat them soon.

Awarded the Best Florist in Singapore, FARM Florist is not showing signs of slowing down!
